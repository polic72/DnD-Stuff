Jamesin

Medium humanoid (Half Elf), Chaotic Good

Armor Class

16 (studded leather)

Hit Points

36 (8d8)

Speed

30 ft.

STR

15 (+2)

DEX

18 (+4)

CON

10 (+0)

INT

14 (+2)

WIS

11 (+0)

CHA

14 (+2)

Saving Throws

Dex +6, Wis +2, Cha +4

Skills

Acrobatics +6, Sleight of Hand +6, Stealth +6

Senses

darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10

Languages

Common, Elvish

Challenge

3 (700 XP)

Fey Ancestry.

Jamesin has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put Jamesin to sleep.

Surprise Attack.

If Jamesin surprises a creature and hits it with an attack during the first round of combat, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) damage from the attack.

Actions

Multiattack.

Jamesin makes three melee attacks: two with his scimitar and one with his dagger. Or Jamesin makes two ranged attacks with his daggers.

Scimitar.

Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) slashing damage.

Dagger.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage.

Bonus Actions

Shadow Stealth.

While in dim light or darkness, Jamesin can take the Hide action as a bonus action.

Reactions

Parry.

Jamesin adds 2 to his AC against one melee attack that would hit him. To do so, Jamesin must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
